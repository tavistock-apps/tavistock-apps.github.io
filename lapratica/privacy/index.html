Privacy Policy

La Pratica · Music Practice Made Fun

Effective date: April 12, 2026

Overview

La Pratica ("the app") is built with your family’s privacy as a top priority. We collect the minimum data necessary to provide the service, and we never sell your data or share it with advertisers.

Microphone Access

La Pratica requests access to your device’s microphone solely to detect instrument sounds during practice sessions. All audio processing happens entirely on your device using Apple’s SoundAnalysis framework.

  • No audio is ever recorded.
  • No audio is ever transmitted off your device.
  • Audio is analyzed in real time and immediately discarded.

You can revoke microphone access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone.

Data We Store on Your Device

All practice data is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults and is never uploaded to any server. This includes:

  • Practice session history (date, duration, quality score)
  • Practice goals and streaks
  • Settings (instrument selection, threshold preferences)
  • Purchase status (Family Pack unlock)

This data is included in standard iOS device backups (iCloud or iTunes) if you have backups enabled.

In-App Purchases

La Pratica offers a one-time in-app purchase called the Family Pack. All purchase transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment information. Purchase status is verified locally via Apple’s StoreKit framework.

Children’s Privacy

La Pratica is designed to be used by children under parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app does not include accounts, profiles, social features, or any data collection that would be subject to COPPA or similar regulations.

Third-Party Services

La Pratica does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash reporting services. The only third-party framework used is Apple’s SoundAnalysis (on-device, no network calls).

Data Deletion

You can delete all practice data at any time from within the app: Settings → Clear All History. Uninstalling the app from your device removes all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
